Women's Center Archive: University of Virginia

Summary Statistics
Number of Records: 431
Year Range: 1954 - 2016
Collection ID: 14
DESCRIPTION
The items in this Collection document cases of reported racial discrimination and violence that appeared in the student newspaper Cavalier Daily from 1954 to at least 2016, including but not limited to events at UVA and in Charlottesville. The inclusion of events outside Charlottesville illuminates the discourse of race carried on in the Cavalier Daily. By including a Collection dedicated to this topic, the creators of this archive hope to facilitate scholarship at the intersection of race and sexual violence.
